# AIDEEN Study Books

This collection is meant to be read as a set of long-form study books, not as short notes.

Target reader:

- someone who wants to understand AI seriously,
- someone who may currently feel weak in mathematics,
- someone who specifically wants to understand AIDEEN from first principles.

Planned structure:

## Volume I — Mathematics Foundations for AI

Purpose:
start from the ground level and build the mathematical language needed for machine learning and modern model architectures.

Scope:

- numbers,
- algebra,
- equations,
- functions,
- graphs,
- vectors,
- matrices,
- linear systems,
- geometric intuition,
- why those ideas matter for AI.
